burger yummy. what else to say :)

Ingredients

  • Burger Buns
  • Burger Patty
  • Sauce
  • Optional Extras (vegetables, extra meat, cheese, etc)

Instructions

  1. Prepare all your ingredients you'll be using
  2. Grill the patty
    • Tip: you can melt the cheese while grilling the patty
  3. Assemble the burger with two buns on top & bottom, patty inside first, then toppings, then sauce
